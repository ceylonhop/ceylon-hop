About Ceylon Hop

From best friends to travel guides

We mapped every corner of this island — for fun, first.

Hi, we’re Roshen and Dasis, co-founders of Ceylon Hop. We’ve been best friends for over 20 years, ever since school. Our shared love for travel has been the foundation of that friendship.

Over the years we organised countless trips for our friends — crafting experiences that were seamless, exciting and unforgettable. Looking back, it feels like we were always meant to do this. Ceylon Hop is here to help others explore Sri Lanka the way we love to: with adventure, joy and ease.

Now, we can’t wait to share that experience with you. Welcome aboard!

Why we started

Getting around Sri Lanka shouldn’t be the hard part.

Figuring out transport here wasn’t always easy. Public transport can be confusing, planning the perfect trip often felt overwhelming, and most options lacked the flexibility to explore at your own pace.

We started Ceylon Hop because we’ve been there — and we wanted to create a service that makes discovering Sri Lanka simple, flexible and stress-free.
