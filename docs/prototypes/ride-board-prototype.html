The honest details

Everything you'd want to ask

How does the board work?

1. Add your name to a list going your way — or start a new one. 2. Share the list; every name added gets it closer to running. 3. Four names in, the van rolls — licensed Ceylon Hop driver, door to door, fare split between everyone.

What happens after I add my name?

Your card gets a hold — $0 charged. If the list reaches 4 names by its deadline, everyone's charged their share and the van is locked in. We email you either way, the moment it happens.

What if the list doesn't fill?

You choose: upgrade to a private car split between whoever signed, move to the next scheduled shared ride, or simply walk away. The hold disappears. You're never charged for a ride that didn't run.

Who's driving?

We are — Ceylon Hop. Licensed drivers and insured air-con vans, the same ones behind our private transfers, rated 5.0 on Tripadvisor. The board finds the people; we do the driving.

Why sign in with Google?

So every name on a list is a real person — no ghosts who never show. Other travellers only ever see your first name and country. Never your email, never your number.

Can I bring a friend?

Yes — take two lines at once. You're charged together, or not at all.

est. 2024 🇱🇰

Why we run this board

We started Ceylon Hop wanting strangers to share vans — it's cheaper for you, lighter on the island, and honestly more fun. But our fixed timetable kept missing the days you actually wanted to travel.

So the board sets the timetable now. You pick the routes and days; when enough names stack up, we send the van. If a list doesn't fill, nobody pays and no van drives empty.

— the Ceylon Hop crew
