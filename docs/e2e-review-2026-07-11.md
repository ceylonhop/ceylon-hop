## Synthesis 1/3 — System / backend-consistency verdict

**Headline:** *Pricing parity is genuinely strong; the request/response API contract is leaky, and a whole product family (loops) plus the shared-service-day rule have no backend agreement.*

### 1. Is the backend the single source of truth? — Mostly yes, and well-guarded
- Prices are canonical in `api/src/quote/rateCard.ts` + the corridor seat prices in `departureRepo.ts`, and the front-end `transfers-data.js` / `routes-data.js` mirrors are **code-generated** (`tools/generate-pricing.mjs`, `@generated:pricing` blocks) with parity/freshness tests. This is the healthiest consistency story in the codebase — a rate change flows one way and the guard catches drift.
- Two small holes in the mirror: `plan.js:118` hand-copies the `$29/$50` floors instead of reading `TRANSFERS.FLOORS` (BQ5), and the front-end mixes prices with derived UI text that the codegen doesn't cover.

### 2. The API contract (customer JS ↔ backend routes) — leaky, several 400/mispricing paths
The engine-authoritative pricing is right, but the *shapes and routing* of requests disagree with the backend in ways a real customer hits:
- **Loops have no backend model (SC3, high):** `type:'loop'` products ($129/$95/$99) post to `/bookings/single`; the engine reprices a 9-stop, multi-day loop as one Airport→last-stop transfer. Either model loops server-side or make them concierge-quote-only.
- **Catalogue shared entries can't resolve (SC4, medium):** stop strings (`'Sigiriya'`, `'Ahangama'`) don't exact-match `CORRIDOR_ROUTES` (`'Sigiriya / Dambulla'`, no Ahangama) → `400 unknown_corridor` unless a `?corridor=` is present.
- **Required departure time can be omitted (SC2, high):** shared payload sends `time: state.dep || undefined`; backend requires it → 400 at the moment of payment.
- **Client-only charges (SC5/SC8):** shared extra-bag `$10/bag` and the Colombo-pickup `$3/seat` surcharge exist on one side only, so quote ≠ charge (and every drift spawns an ops mismatch task).
- **No idempotency (SC6, medium):** front-end never sends the `Idempotency-Key` the backend implements → retries/cold-starts create duplicate drafts and re-hold seats.
- **Display drift (SC7):** catalogue shared routes default to a 7:30am slot while their corridors depart 08:00.

### 3. Rule consistency across surfaces — one live divergence
- **Shared Wed & Sat service days (DD1, high):** enforced on neither surface on `main` (PR #32 unmerged); `transfers-data.js` still advertises "Daily". FE and BE agree — but agree on the *wrong* thing.

### Verdict & priority
- **Keep doing:** the pricing codegen + parity guard — extend it to cover `plan.js` floors and, ideally, the corridor stop-name lists so FE/BE names can't drift (SC4).
- **Fix next (contract):** give loops a real backend path or gate them (SC3); require `time` client-side (SC2); send `Idempotency-Key` (SC6); reconcile extra-bag/pickup (SC5/SC8).
- **Fix (rule):** merge PR #32 + land the FE calendar/"Daily"→"Wed & Sat" copy (DD1).
- **Net:** the *money math* is consistent; the *plumbing around it* is where FE and BE disagree.
