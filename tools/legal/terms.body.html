2. OUR SERVICES

Ceylon Hop provides private ground transport across Sri Lanka. We offer three ways to travel:

  • Private transfers — a private, air-conditioned vehicle (car or van) between any two points you choose, door to door, at a fixed price. A single booking may include several stops or legs.
  • Shared seats — a per-seat fare on selected popular routes that run on set service days. Seats are limited, subject to availability, and confirmed only once your booking is paid.
  • Chauffeur-guide trips — a private vehicle with a driver-guide who stays with you for the whole journey, priced per day. You arrange and book your own accommodation; we suggest options but do not sell or guarantee them.

Each vehicle class has a maximum number of passengers and bags. Please book a vehicle suited to your group size and luggage — we may be unable to carry more than the stated capacity.

Travelling with minors: passengers under 18 must travel with a parent, spouse, or legal guardian (at least one adult per two minors). Identification and proof of age may be requested.

7. REFUNDS & CANCELLATIONS

Your cancellation terms depend on how you travel:

Point-to-point transfers (single or multi-leg)

  • Free cancellation and unlimited changes up to 24 hours before departure — a full refund of the ticket price.
  • Within 24 hours of departure, a no show, or after departure: no refund is provided.

Chauffeur-guide trips (a retained driver-guide for the whole journey)

  • More than 10 days before the start date: free cancellation with a full refund.
  • Within 10 days of the start date: 80% refund of the trip price.
  • Within 7 days of the start date: 60% refund.
  • Within 2 days of the start date: 40% refund.
  • Within 24 hours of the start date, a no show, or after the trip begins: no refund is provided.

Cancellation Requests:

  • Must be made via WhatsApp (+94779669662) or email ( hello@ceylonhop.com ) by the cardholder.
  • Phone calls or other forms of communication will not be considered valid cancellation requests.

Flexibility After Departure:
