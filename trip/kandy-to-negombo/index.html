Kandy to Negombo is the classic pre-flight run — about 115 km and 2 hours 50 minutes down through the Kadugannawa hairpins, door to door, with room for a Pinnawala stop if you set off early.

  • Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage detour near Kegalle
  • Kadugannawa pass with views of Bible Rock
  • Spice gardens around Mawanella
  • Royal Botanic Gardens at Peradeniya
  • Kandy Lake and the Temple of the Sacred Tooth

Kandy to Negombo — questions

How long does the Kandy to Negombo transfer take?

The drive is about 2h 51m on 115 km of road. Your driver takes the fastest safe route and can add stops along the way.

How much is a taxi from Kandy to Negombo?

A private car is from $58 and an air-conditioned van (up to 6 people) from $105, fixed and door to door — the price you see is the price you pay. A daily shared seat is from $19 per person.

Is there a cheaper shared option?

Yes — this route runs on our Airport–Cultural Triangle shared service (Daily). A single seat is from $19, ideal for solo travellers and couples happy to share.

Can we stop along the way?

Of course. A private transfer is door to door and yours for the trip — tell your driver where you'd like to stop for photos, lunch or a quick sight and they'll build it in.

How do I book the Kandy to Negombo transfer?

Get an instant fixed price and book online, or message us on WhatsApp and we'll arrange it. You pay securely online to confirm your booking — the price you see is the price you pay.
