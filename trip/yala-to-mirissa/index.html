Yala to Mirissa

Private transfer & shared ride — 155 km, about 3h 17m door to door.

Private carfrom $69
AC van (up to 6)from $92
Shared seatfrom $16
See prices & book Chat on WhatsApp

This one hugs the coast almost the whole way: 155 km and about 3 hours 17 minutes from the lodges around Tissamaharama past Hambantota's salt pans, Tangalle's coves and Dondra's lighthouse to Mirissa. A private car can detour to the Hummanaya blowhole; the shared ride south leaves on Wed & Sat at 8:00am.

  • Lagoons and salt pans around Bundala, busy with birdlife
  • Tangalle's quiet crescent beaches
  • Hummanaya blowhole near Kudawella
  • Dondra Head lighthouse at the island's southernmost point
  • Mirissa's whale-watching harbour

Yala to Mirissa — questions

How long does the Yala to Mirissa transfer take?

The drive is about 3h 17m on 155 km of road. Your driver takes the fastest safe route and can add stops along the way.

How much is a taxi from Yala to Mirissa?

A private car is from $69 and an air-conditioned van (up to 6 people) from $92, fixed and door to door — the price you see is the price you pay. A shared seat is from 16 per person.

Is there a cheaper shared option?

Yes — this route runs on our Yala–South coast shared service (Wed & Sat). A single seat is from $16, ideal for solo travellers and couples happy to share.

Can we stop along the way?

Of course. A private transfer is door to door and yours for the trip — tell your driver where you'd like to stop for photos, lunch or a quick sight and they'll build it in.

How do I book the Yala to Mirissa transfer?

Get an instant fixed price and book online, or message us on WhatsApp and we'll arrange it. You pay securely online to confirm your booking — the price you see is the price you pay.
